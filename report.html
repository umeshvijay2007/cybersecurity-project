What We Collect Automatically

When you submit, CalmTrace automatically captures the following metadata alongside your report — no manual action required.

  • 🌐 IP Address — Source IP of the session (simulated for privacy)
  • 🕐 Timestamp — ISO 8601 UTC time of report submission
  • 🔗 DNS Logs — Simulated suspicious domain queries linked to incident
  • 🔏 SHA-256 Hash — Tamperproof fingerprint of the complete record
🔐

Privacy Guarantee

CalmTrace stores only metadata and your submitted details. All data remains on your local machine. Nothing is transmitted to external servers. Contact email is optional and used only for follow-up.

Still in Danger?

If you believe you are actively under attack, immediately:

  • 🔌 Disconnect from the network if possible
  • 📸 Screenshot any suspicious activity
  • 📞 Contact your local cybercrime authority
  • 🔒 Change passwords from a safe device
